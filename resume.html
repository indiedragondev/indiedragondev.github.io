Fallen Seoul

This game takes place in the remnants of Seoul, South Korea, sometime in the distant future. It follows the perspective of a mechanic who worked for a corrupt robotics company which caused the destruction. He’s the only human left in the city, and will have to gather upgrades around a linear map to upgrade his robot companion in order to reach new areas, and overcome obstacles.

You're... Who?

This game is a platformer where you are a criminal trying to escape a notorious dungeon. Meet charming prisoners, face perils, and escape. Yet... are you the same person you once were?

Alms of Witheria

You are a Mage collecting Light-Matter to give to the Poor. Collect Light-Matter, avoid Dark-Matter. How high can you get your score? You have a bowl that collects Light-Matter.
